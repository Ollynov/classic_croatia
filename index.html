ABOUT CASA COLIBRI

The real Croatia experience.

Our vacation house was recently built in 2018, and features 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a pool. We are in a great location, nested in the wine region, with accessibility to both local villages and the sea within 15 minutes. It has both air conditioning and heating, but nothing beats chilling outside by the pool.

Marija Dugandzija


I live in Zagreb, and have been vacationing to the Istra region for over 10 years now. My family and I have traveled all along the beautiful coast of Croatia, and we have fallen in love the most with Istria. It has a certain country charm, yet with a beatiful coastline, a combo which can rarely be found. Much of the economy in the area is small business driven, from vineyards to olive plantations, and it still maintains a "local" vibe, and is not as touristey as other parts. I love vacationing here with my family, and love to welcome guests from all over the world to this small yet special corner of the Mediterranean.
